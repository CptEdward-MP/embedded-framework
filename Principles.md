# Engineering Principles

## 1. Infrastructure belongs in the framework. Behavior belongs in the application.

## 2. File higher in the abstraction layer includes the one below it and never vis-versa.

## 3. Files must be reusable for same application in different places with ease.

## 4. Files must not be changed entirely , just allow ease of adding improvements.

## 5. There is no firmware , untill documented.

## 6. There is no firmware , untill examples are demostrated.

## 7. Each piece of firmware must have specific and well defined responsiblies and never share them with   other pieces of firmware. 

## 8. Platform-specific code must remain isolated from application logic.

## 9. Every dependency should have a clear purpose.
